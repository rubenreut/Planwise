Planwise Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Planwise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our AI scheduling assistant app.

Information We Don't Collect

We designed Planwise with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not store your conversations
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties

Data Storage

Your Calendar Data: All your events and schedules are stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We cannot access this data.

AI Conversations: When you chat with our AI assistant:

  • Messages are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • We do not store conversation history on our servers
  • Each conversation is isolated and not linked to you personally
  • OpenAI processes requests according to their privacy policy

Third-Party Services

Planwise uses the following services:

  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered scheduling assistance. Messages are processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • CloudKit: For syncing your data across your devices. Handled by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • StoreKit: For subscription management. No payment information is stored by us

Data Security

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Your data is encrypted on your device
  • iCloud sync uses end-to-end encryption
  • We cannot access your calendar data or personal information

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync in Settings to stop syncing
  • All your data remains yours

Children's Privacy

Planwise is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
